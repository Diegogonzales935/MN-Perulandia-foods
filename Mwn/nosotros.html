We connect the Peruvian countryside with the world, ensuring quality, compliance, and business relationships built on trust.

Growing Together

OUR SUPPLIERS ARE PART OF THE TEAM

Peruvian international trade in agricultural products can be challenging without control over the entire supply chain, putting in risk the viability of the business.

At MN PERU FOODS, we participate in the entire supply chain, making the experience simpler and safer. We do this with passion and dedication, as our goal is to build long-term relationships with our customers and suppliers.

Present at the Source

Our close presence in the field allows us to understand every harvest and ensure the quality our customers expect.

MN PERU FOODS is a Peruvian women-owned company rooted in the field. For more than a decade, we have built lasting relationships with growers, working together to share the richness of Peru's harvests with the world while creating opportunities for sustainable growth.

Being present in the field is at the heart of what we do. By working closely with growers throughout each growing season, we gain a deep understanding of every harvest and ensure the quality and reliability our customers expect. Beyond Peru, we partner with like-minded producers who share our commitment to sustainable growth and responsible sourcing.

Today, MN PERU FOODS is more than a company—it is a celebration of Peru's people, its land, and its agricultural heritage. Every harvest reflects our commitment to preserving what makes our country unique while creating opportunities for future generations.
